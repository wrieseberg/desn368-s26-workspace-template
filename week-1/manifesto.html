Designer-Coder Manifesto

Clear hierarchy improves understanding and reduces confusion.

h2 Is Used for Subheadings

This section is used for main subheadings that organize the biggest parts of the page.

h3 Is Used for Smaller Subsections

This section is used to break down a main section into smaller, more specific parts.

h4 Is Used for More Specific Details

This section is used to add more detail within a subsection.

h5 Is Used for Smaller Supporting Points

This section is used to highlight smaller details that support the main idea.

h6 Is Used for the Smallest Details

This section is used for the most minor details or final points.
