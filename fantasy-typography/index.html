The Thornwood · Western Border of Edrane

The First Night of the Long Rain

The bell rang from beneath the forest floor just as the rain began. Not above the trees,
where belonged, and not from the village tower, which had fallen silent years before. It
rang from deep under the roots, slow and heavy, as if the earth itself had remembered
an old warning.

Liora stopped at the edge of the Thornwood with her lantern held low. The flame bent
toward trees, though there was no wind. Every branch ahead of her was wrapped in
black thorns, andevery thorn carried a drop of rain that shone like a watching eye.
Behind her, the road home had already disappeared into mist.

Her grandmother had told her never to answer a bell in the forest. Bells called names.
Bells openeddoors. Bells woke things that had been buried for the safety of kings and
children. But when the sound came again, Liora heard her own name inside it, clear as
breath against her ear.

Typography Analysis

I chose this version because:

I chose fantasy because, I love all things mystical which goes right along with
fantasy.

The feeling I want the typography to create is:

I want the typography to create a calm, serene feeling, like being deep in the forest.

The fonts, sizes, spacing, and hierarchy I chose help the reader because:

I wanted the body text to create easy reading. i spaced the letters a bit, shortened the
lines, and added extra space between lines. I made the most important things bigger/
bolder. I chose a foresty color palet.
