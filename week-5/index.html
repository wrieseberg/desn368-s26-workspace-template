Her Story

Born Alicia Beth Moore, she grew up in a home
shaped by ongoing conflict between her parents,
an experience that gave her a lot to say and helped
shape her outspoken voice.

She built her career on refusing to fit expectations,
using her platform to speak out on equality, LGBTQ+
rights, and becoming a voice for those often unheard.
