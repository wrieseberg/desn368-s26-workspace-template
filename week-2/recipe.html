White Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 3½ cups flour
  • 1 tsp. baking soda
  • ¾ tsp. salt
  • 1⅓ cups butter softened
  • 1¼ cups granulated sugar
  • 1 cup firmly packed light brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 tsps. pure vanilla extract
  • 1 package (12 ounces) white chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F
  2. Mix flour, baking soda, and salt in medium bowl. Set aside.
  3. Beat butter and sugars in large bowl with electric mixer on medium
    speed until light and fluffy
  4. Add eggs and vanilla; mix well.
  5. Gradually beat in flour mixture on low speed until mixed well.
  6. Stir i chocolate chips.
  7. Drop by rounded Tablespoon about 2 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheet.
  8. Bake 8 to 10 minutes or until lightly browned.
  9. Cool on baking sheet for 1 minute. Transfer cookies from baking sheet to wire
    rack to cool completely.
